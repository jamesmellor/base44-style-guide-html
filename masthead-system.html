Level 1 — Top-level

Home / Open Days

  • ~480px tall; full photo split
  • Dark navy, maximum visual weight
  • No breadcrumb — you are at the root

Level 2 — Section landing

Art & Design, Music, Business…

  • ~300–380px tall; light, blobs
  • Breadcrumb as a separate bar below nav
  • Stat cards or image in right column

Level 3 — Sub-pages

Courses (3A) · Everything else (3B)

  • Breadcrumb always inside the masthead
  • 3A: deep + photo; degree type + section badges
  • 3B: compact light; section-colour strip + section tag
